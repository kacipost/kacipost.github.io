Eye Movement Exercise





The Eye Exercise was a project completed during the MIT xPro Full-Stack Engineering with MERN Professional Certification Course. In this exercise the main learning components were combining HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to create a web page with two eyes that follow your mouse movement around the screen.
